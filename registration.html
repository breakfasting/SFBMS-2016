Registration Info

2016 SFBMS, EAJS, CSSBMR會議邀請貴賓及贊助廠商免註冊。

Invited guests and sponsors are free to attend the meeting and do not need to register.


其它参加者請於會場憑學術機關/組織有效証件註冊並繳交新台幣 1000元註冊費* (不退費)。

All other attendees register on site with valid academic ID and registration fee NTD 1,000 dollars* (Non refundable).


*註冊費用含入場名牌、議程手冊及二日份午餐。

*Registration fee includes an ID pass, a meeting brochure and two-day lunch tickets.
